Decolonise East Asian Studies

During my time at the University of Manchester, I founded Decolonise East Asian Studies, a campaign aimed at raising awareness of East Asian advocacy as well as decolonise the current curriculum.

We have been successful in providing students with talks on topics such as the British Chinese diaspora, East Asian womenhood, working with Jamie Chi of Queer China UK, Feiya Hu of Ru Informed (formerly Racism Unmasked), Dear Asian Youth London, CACH-ALL, esea contemporary gallery as well as individuals such as Denise Kwan and Dr Ed Pulford from the University of Manchester Chinese Studies department.

Due to support from Dr Pulford, content regarding British East Asian experience, migration, stereotypes and social issues is now part of the Introduction to Chinese Studies unit that first year students will study.
