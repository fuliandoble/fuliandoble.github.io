My music

I am classical singer with a Certificate in Higher Education from the prestigious RNCM, as well as ARSM in both piano and voice.

I have sung in many choirs in the past such as; RNCM Symphony Chorus, BBC Youth Choir and St Mary's Choir, Conwy. With St Mary's, I have regularly appeared on the Radio Wales recordings from the church. As a soloist, I performed at the prestigious Rhyl Music Club and various charities including Cruse Bereavement Care and St David's Hospice. I have also held solo concerts for the Conwy Classical Music Festival which is available to listen to on my personal Youtube.

Currently I sing Soprano 1 in Schola Apium, a chamber choir based in Manchester.
