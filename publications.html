  • How much of a challenge does gender inequality pose to China’s rise socially and economically?

During my final year at the University of Manchester, I studied a unit called 'Why China Matters' which was run by the Manchester China Institute. My blogpost article was one of the ones chosen to be published on their website. Titled "How much of a challenge does gender inequality pose to China’s rise socially and economically?", my blogpost explores the idea of gender inequality, unpacking the key issues raised from the documentary 'Sparrow Village.'
